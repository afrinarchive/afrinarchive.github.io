Summaries

I. Summary from TirejAfrin Site (English) of Abû Ke'bê Xerbî

According to the book "جبل الكرد" "Mountain of the Kurds (Afrin) geographical study" by Dr. Mohammed Abdo Ali:

Ebû Ke'ibê, أبو كعب /225n - 180m - 5km/:

  • Named after the shrine of "أبو كعب" (Abu Ka'b) the Yazidi located there.
  • Two sections, western: consists of about twenty families. ==And eastern: has one family.==

And according to the book: Afrin... Her River and Green Hills by writer Abdulrahman Mohammed from Qatma village:

أبو كعب غربي (Abu Ka'b Western): A farm in Afrin River valley, belonging to Cindirês District, Afrin, Aleppo Governorate.

It is a small farm located on a fertile agricultural plateau on the right bank of Afrin River, about 10km from the town of Cindirês. To the north it is bordered by a fertile agricultural plain and a seasonal stream planted with olive trees and Al-Ramadiya village. To the south, 500m away, is Afrin River valley and Jalma village in the far south. To the west by a fertile agricultural plain planted with olive trees and Hajiler village. To the east by a fertile agricultural plain and Afrin River valley and Abu Ka'b Eastern village and Western Zallaqa and the shrine of أبو كعب (Abu Ka'b) at a distance of 1km. ==The number of its houses is 35 houses== and its ==age is about 50 years. It is a village of recent era, its houses are concrete stone.== Only a paved dirt road is available, not asphalted. The village drinks from artesian wells located beside the houses. Its inhabitants work in cultivating grains and olives with irrigation and rain-fed from pumping from the green Afrin River in summer, and cotton and tobacco in addition to raising sheep, cattle, and goats.
