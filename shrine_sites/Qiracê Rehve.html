Qiracê Rehve

General Information

Type

Ziyareta

Nahiya (Subdistrict)

Şiyê

Village or Nearby Villages

Anqelê, Senarê

Qiracê Rehve is a shrine between Anqelê and Senarê in the Şiyê sub-district.

Physical & Landscape Features

It lies on the slope below the hill of Til Carnas (Tilê Cirnasê). The slope is covered with old graves. According to a local elder, the hill takes its name from a king called Cirnas whose palace once stood there. The elder says the graves covering the Qiracê Rehve slope probably belonged to the people of that palace on the hill.1
