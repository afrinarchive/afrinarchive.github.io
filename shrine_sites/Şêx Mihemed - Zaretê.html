Village or Nearby Villages

Zaretê

Şêx Mihemed, known as Şêx Mihemed el-Kûfî, was a shrine of Zaretê in the Efrîn sub-district. The village name, Çetel Ziyaretê, means "two shrines," after this shrine and the nearby Şêx Yûsif el-Kûfî. The two men were kinsmen who fell as martyrs in a great battle on this spot and were buried here, and a shrine with two domes was raised over them. In 2013, when ISIS entered the village, both shrines were blown up.1

This is one of several separate shrines in the Afrin region that share the name. The others are:

  • Şêx Mihemed - Çema
  • Şêx Mihemed - Kefersefrê
  • Şêx Mihemed - Kifêrê
  • Şêx Mihemed - Miskê
  • Şêx Mihemed - Sêmala
  • Şêx Mihemed - Tetera
