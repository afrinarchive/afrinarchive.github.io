Mezara Hecî Yûsif is a shrine of Sêwiya.

Dedicated Figure & Historical Roots

East of Sêwiya, at Bîra Kêrbeze, also known among Europeans as Kêlbaze, a fierce battle took place between the Ayyubid army and Crusader attackers. Hecî Yûsif, commander of the Ayyubid army, was killed in that battle, and people of the region still visit his tomb.1
