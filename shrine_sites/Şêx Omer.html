Axcelê

Şêx Omer is a shrine of Axcelê in the Cindires sub-district.

Ritual Practices & Beliefs

The shrine lies to the north of the village. The narrator of the Ax û Welat visit to Axcelê (Episode 969) says the villagers formerly visited it to pray for rain to fall.1
