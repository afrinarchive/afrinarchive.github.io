Şêx Mihemed - Miskê

General Information

Also Known As

Şêx Mihemed (Miskê), Şêx Mihemed (Miskê) shrine, Sheikh Mohammed shrine (Miske)

Type

Ziyareta

Nahiya (Subdistrict)

Cindires

Village or Nearby Villages

Miske Jerin

Şêx Mihemed is a shrine at Miske in the Cindires sub-district, with a sacred tree standing nearby.

This is one of several separate shrines in the Afrin region that share the name. The others are:

  • Şêx Mihemed - Çema
  • Şêx Mihemed - Kefersefrê
  • Şêx Mihemed - Kifêrê
  • Şêx Mihemed - Sêmala
  • Şêx Mihemed - Tetera
  • Şêx Mihemed - Zaretê

Map and Location

Dedicated Figure & Historical Roots

The shrine is presumed dedicated to a local Sufi saint, since neither Alevi nor Yezidi sources mention it.1

Current Status

The shrine was partially destroyed in October 2018, and the sacred tree nearby was also destroyed. The damage was photographed by March 2019 and is visible on satellite imagery.2
