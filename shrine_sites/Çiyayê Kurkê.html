Village or Nearby Villages

Xirab Simaq

Çiyayê Kurkê is the shrine of Mount Kurkê at Xirab Simaq, where the village sits on Kurkê Mountain.1

Physical & Landscape Features

The shrine is on the top of Kurkê, and the mountain was named and became known because of it.2
