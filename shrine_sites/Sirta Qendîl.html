Sirta Qendîl

General Information

Also Known As

Sirta Qendîl shrine, Kevrê Qendîl, Rock of Qendil, Sirta Qendil

Type

Ziyareta

Nahiya (Subdistrict)

Efrîn

Village or Nearby Villages

Xelnêrê

Sirta Qendîl is a shrine of Xelnêrê in the Efrîn sub-district, one of the three shrines of the village alongside Qere Baba and Sare Qîzê.1

Physical & Landscape Features

The shrine is a rock at the top of the jagged mountain above the village, also called Kevrê Qendîl, the Rock of Qendil, qendîl meaning candle or lamp. The rock stands between two shrines, about one hundred and fifty metres from one of them, and the whole area is known by the names of Qere Baba and the Rock of Qendil. The villagers say they do not know where the name came from.2
