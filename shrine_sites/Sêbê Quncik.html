Sêbê Quncik

General Information

Also Known As

Sêbê Quncik shrine, Ziyareta Sêbê Quncik, Sabê Quncik

Type

Ziyareta

Nahiya (Subdistrict)

Reco

Village or Nearby Villages

Ĥêcîka Jorin, Ĥêcîka Jêrin

Sêbê Quncik is a shrine of Ĥêcîka Jorin and Ĥêcîka Jêrin in the Reco sub-district.

Physical & Landscape Features

It is one of two shrines, the other being Sêbikê, that lie to the west of the villages.1
