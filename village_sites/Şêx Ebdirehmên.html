Summaries

I. Summary from TirejAfrin Site of Şêx Ebdirehmên (English)

According to the book 'جبل الكرد' 'Mountain of the Kurds' (عفرين) Geographic Study by Dr. Mohammed Abdo Ali (الدكتور محمد عبدو علي):

Kanî Gewrkê (شيخ عبدالرحمن), Sheikh Abdulrahman (شيخ عبدالرحمن) /1040n - 7km - 270m/:

  • Kanî Kawrk (كاني كاورك): meaning "the white spring", while Sheikh Abdulrahman (شيخ عبدالرحمن): is the name of the shrine near it, known by the name of the companion Abdulrahman ibn Awf (عبدالرحمن بن عوف).
  • A medium-sized village located on the eastern bank of Sheikh Abdulrahman valley (وادي شيخ عبدالرحمن) coming from the north. It is one of the villages of Sido Mimi aghas (آغوات سيدو ميمي) and they still inhabit it.

According to the book: 'عفرين' 'Afrin'.... Its River and Green Hills by writer Abdulrahman Mohammed (عبدالرحمن محمد) from Qatmeh village (قرية قطمه):

Sheikh Abdulrahman (شيخ عبدالرحمن): A village in Kurdish mountain (جبل الكرد) belonging to Cindires sub-district (ناحية جندرس), Afrin region (منطقة عفرين), Aleppo governorate (محافظة حلب).

It is a small and modern village located in the northeastern part of Kane Kurk spring valley (وادي نبع كعنكورك) which flows south to feed Afrin river (نهر عفرين) at 2km distance. The village is 8km northeast of Cindires town (بلدة جندرس).

It is bordered to the north by fertile agricultural plains and Kane Kurk valley (وادي كعنكورك) and Sheikh Abdulrahman shrine (مزار الشيخ عبد الرحمن) and Qara Bash village (قرية قرة باش) and Julqan (جولقان), to the south by Kane Kurk valley (وادي كعنكورك) and the road (Afrin City (عفرين) - Cindires (جندرس)) and Tell Hemo village (قرية تل حمو) and Faririye (الفريرية), to the west by Kane Kurk valley (وادي كعنكورك) and Tell Athri (تل أثري) and Qurba village (قرية قوربه) and Qujha Man (قوجه مان), and to the east by fertile plains planted with olive trees and Andriye farm (مزرعة العندرية) and Tillif village (قرية تلليف).

The number of houses is about 60 houses and its age is about 100 years (100عاماً). Its old houses are stone and clay with flat wooden roofs, and the modern ones are concrete that expanded northward and southward. It has an electricity network and a water network connected to wells dug in the neighboring Miske Upper village (قرية مسكة فوقانية). It has an elementary school and an olive press located in the western part of the village belonging to Mr. Hassan Hamada (السيد حسن حمادة) and his sons and their roots from Qatma village (قرية قطمة). It connects with the sub-district center and the region by paved road.

Most of its residents work in olive, grain and legume cultivation rain-fed and irrigated on an area of (665 hectares) and summer vegetables irrigated from wells (10 hectares), in addition to raising sheep and goats. There is a small farm in the western part of the village belonging to Hassan Hamada (حسن حمادة) and next to it a modern olive press. It was previously a farm for farmers of the Sido Mimi agha family (عائلة سيدو ميمي آغا) from the nearby Julqan village (قرية جولقان).

Among the scientific and cultural figures are Dr. Mustafa Sido (الدكتور مصطفى سيدو) - Engineer Kamal Haj Abdo (المهندس كمال حاج عبدو) - Dr. Brivana Sido (الدكتورة بريفان سيدو) - and many intellectuals and holders of scientific degrees. The village mayor is Murad Izzat Murad (مراد عزت مراد).
