Summaries

I. Summary from TirejAfrin Site of Uga (English)

According to the book 'جبل الكرد' 'Mountain of the Kurds' / Afrin City (عفرين) / Geographical Study:

Uga (عوكانلي), Ukanli (عوكان) /2488N - 465H - 6km - 660m/:

  • Uga (عوغا): It is believed to be derived from a local proper name.
  • It is a small village located on a mountainous elevation.

According to the book 'عفرين .... نهرها ورواطيها الخضراء' 'Afrin.... Its River and Green Hills':

Ukanli (عوكانلي): A village in Kurdistan Mountain (جبل الكرد) that belongs to Bulbul District (ناحية بلبل), Afrin City region (منطقة عفرين), Aleppo Governorate (محافظة حلب).

It is a small village located at the southern foothills of a calcareous mountain in the northern part of the Kurdistan Mountain (جبل الكرد) mass. Streams surround it from the south and west, and it overlooks its fertile agricultural lands in the southeast. It is 6km southeast of Bulbul Town (بلدة بلبل). To the north it is bordered by a torrent valley and mountainous elevation and Bulbul Town (بلدة بلبل) and Qorne (قورنه), to the south by a slope planted with olive trees and Qurt Oshaghi Village (قرية قورت أوشاغي) and Qash Oshaghi Village (قرية قاش أوشاغي), to the west by a very deep torrent valley and Sharqiyanli Village (قرية شرقيانلي) 2km away, and to the east by a slope and torrent valley and the Bulbul-Maidanki (بلبل ميدانكي) road and Jarjali Village (قرية جارجالي).

The number of its houses is 75 houses, age about 400 years. Its old houses are stone-clay with flat wooden roofs, and the modern ones are concrete stone. It has an electricity network and an elementary school. It drinks from a water network connected to artesian wells west of the village and from cisterns that collect rainwater. Most of its inhabitants work in olive, grain, vine, and legume cultivation on an area of 465 hectares and in sheep and goat raising. The road from it to Bulbul Town (بلدة بلبل) is paved and the side road to the village is paved dirt. It is followed by Al-Jarura Farm (مزرعة الجارورة). The village is considered one of the oldest villages there, where there are olive trees on the southern side aged more than 150 years according to village residents' accounts.

Village Mayor: Sheikh Nasan Miqdad (شيخ نعسان مقداد)
