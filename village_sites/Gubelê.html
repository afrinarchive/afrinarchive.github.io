Possible Village Name Meaning of Gubelê

Gabelek means 'spotted bull'; the early village residents owned spotted bulls that grazed in the area, so the village became known by them. It is also the name of a small bird.

Summaries

I. Summary from TirejAfrin Site of Gubelê (English)

According to the book 'جبل الكرد' 'Mountain of the Kurds' / Afrin City / Geographic Study:

Gabeleka (گابلك) (al-Qatira القطيرة) /522N - 460m/:

  • Gabelek (گابلك): Its meaning is "spotted bull", the early village residents owned spotted bulls that grazed in those areas, so the village became known by them. It is also the name of a small bird.
  • A small village on the southern slope of a plateau that descends toward the valley south of the village.

According to the book: Afrin... Her River and Green Hills by writer Abdulrahman Mohammed from Qatmeh village:

Koblik (كوبلك): A village located in Kurdish Mountain (جبل الكرد) belonging to Sharran District (ناحية شران), Afrin (منطقة عفرين), Aleppo Governorate (محافظة حلب).

It is a small village located on the western slope of a limestone plateau in the northern part of Sam'an Mountain (جبل سمعان) and a water stream passes through it heading west toward the Afrin River (نهر عفرين) valley with clayey soil. It is bounded to the north by a mountain chain planted with forest trees and the village of Ali Bazanli (علي بازانلي) and Medanki (ميدانكي). To the south by a deep valley and mountain chain planted with olive, grape and walnut trees and the Medanki-Bulbul road (طريق ميدانكي- بلبل) and the village of Haloubi Small (حلوبي صغير) and Matinli (ماتينلي). To the east valley and plain and mountain chain and the village of Khirbet Sharran (خربة شران) and the town of Sharran (شران). To the west valley planted with walnut and olive trees and the valley of Haloubi Large (حلوبي كبير) named Qara Jarnah (قره جرنه) and the village of Haloubi Large (حلوبي كبير) and the Afrin River (نهر عفرين). The number of its houses is about 35 houses and its age is 250 years. Its old houses are clay-stone with flat stone roofs and the modern construction around them extends toward the east and west on both sides of the public road. It has an electricity network and state drinking water and telephone and an elementary school and a paved road branches from the public road to Bulbul (بلبل) reaching the village center. The residents work in olive, grain and grape cultivation and livestock raising and it is considered one of the beautiful villages in the region due to the abundance of trees surrounding it. The number of its population as of 31/12/2004 is (504 people).

Village mayor: Ali Abdul Qader (علي عبدالقادر)
