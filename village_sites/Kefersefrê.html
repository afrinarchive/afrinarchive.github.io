The village of Kafr Safra, located approximately five kilometers from the Jandires district, has a history that its residents trace back to 37 AD, an age determined by its local spring. According to an 85-year-old resident, the village is known by two names: one for its goodness, and another as an "oppressor and oppressed village," a term he attributes to the historical departure of its wealthy class.
