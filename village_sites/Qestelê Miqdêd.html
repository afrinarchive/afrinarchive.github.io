From "The Mountains of the Kurds (Afrin Region): a Comprehensive Geographical Study"

Miqdêd Qestelê, قسطل مقداد (Qastal Miqdad), Mazra'at Sharqa - Al-Sharqa /388/ – 7 km - 560 m/

  • Qastal: a name for a spring and a house that existed on the western side of the old archaeological hill located east of the village. And Miqdad is the name of one of the old inhabitants of the village, and his descendants still live there.
  • A large village on the eastern slope of Jebel Sharqa It has small workshops and some building material shops, farming implements, and a new school. It is an old village established on the ruins of an old settlement, and it is developing rapidly. The old village is on the peak of the hill of Gazê, 1 km west of it. Its inhabitants moved to its current location.

Taken from 246, 247 of the Arabic edition of:

Ali, Mohammed Abdo. Jibal al-Kurd (Mintaqat Afrin): Dirasah Jughrafiyah Shamilah [The Mountains of the Kurds (Afrin Region): A Comprehensive Geographical Study]. Self-published, 2014.

Summaries

I. Summary from TirejAfrin Site of Qestelê Miqdêd (English)

According to the book 'جبل الكرد' 'Mountain of the Kurds' / Afrin City (عفرين) / Geographical Study:

Qestelê Miqdêd (قسطل مقداد), Sharqan Farm - Al-Mashriqa (مزرعة شرقان– المشرقة) /388N–7km- 560m/:

  • Qastal (قسطل): Name of an old well and water source located on the western side of the archaeological tell east of the village. Miqdad (مقداد) is the name of one of the old inhabitants of the village, and he still has descendants in it.
  • A large village located on the eastern slope of Sharqiya Mountain (جبل شرقيا). It has small workshops for some building materials, blacksmithing, and agricultural machinery repair. It is a modern village established since the late fifth decade of the twentieth century, and the old village was located on Gazê Summit (قمة غازي) 1km west of it, and its inhabitants migrated to its current location.

According to the book 'عفرين .... نهرها ورواطيها الخضراء' 'Afrin.... Its River and Green Hills':

Qastal Miqdad (قسطل مقداد): A village in Kurdistan Mountain (جبل الكرد) that belongs to Bulbul District (ناحية بلبل), Afrin City region (منطقة عفرين), Aleppo Governorate (محافظة حلب).

It is a very large village located in a low valley and on both sides of the Bulbul-Maidanki (بلبل ميدانكي) road. To the north it is bordered by a mountain chain planted with olive trees and Ukanli Valley Village (قرية عوكانلي وادي) which passes by the village side, to the south by a valley and depression and Kutanli Village (قرية كوتانلي) "Al-Mudallala" (المدللة), to the west by a mountain chain planted with olive trees and Sharqiyanli Village (قرية شرقيانلي), and to the east by a high mountain chain and Sheikh Khurus Village (قرية شيخ خوروس).

It has an electricity network and state drinking water. A paved road passes through its center heading to Bulbul (بلبل) and neighboring villages. It has an elementary and preparatory school and a mosque and some commercial and service shops and repair workshops because the village is located on the easy public road. This village and its lands belong to Sharqiyanli Village (قرية شرقيانلي), and it is more like a dependency to it. Its inhabitants are from Sharqiyanli Village (قرية شرقيانلي) and some neighboring villages because of its location on the public road. The number of houses is about 150 houses, and the village age is recent, about 35 years or slightly more. The main source is agriculture and other service work. It has a telephone service center.
